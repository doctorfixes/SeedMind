The process

How SeedMind works

Three simple steps. One continuously improving companion.

  1. 1

    You share an idea

    Type anything — a half-formed thought, a question, or a problem you're stuck on. SeedMind listens without judgement.

  2. 2

    SeedMind generates & learns

    The LLM produces structured ideas and extracts preference signals. Your growth ring is updated in real time.

  3. 3

    Ideas get sharper over time

    As your ring matures, every prompt is enriched with your context — so responses become more accurate, relevant, and inspiring.
